Banana Protein Shake

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas
  • 1 tbs of peanut butter
  • One scoop of protein powder of your choice
  • Lots of ice
  • 250ml of full cream milk

Steps

  1. Peel bananas
  2. Break bananas into small pieces and place into blender
  3. Put in your peanut butter, ice and protein powder
  4. Pour in milk
  5. Blend until your smoothie is nice and smooth (time will vary depending on make and model of blender
  6. Enjoy!
